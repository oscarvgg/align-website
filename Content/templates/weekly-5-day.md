## What's included

- **5-day layout** (Monday through Friday)
- Hourly rows covering the full workday
- One page per week
- Letter and A4 friendly

## How to plan your week

1. **Sunday evening: print one sheet** and fill in your fixed commitments.
2. **Block recurring meetings, classes, and routines** first.
3. **Schedule your most important work** during your peak energy window each day.
4. **Spread admin and shallow work** across afternoons.
5. **Friday: review** — what worked, what slipped, what to carry forward.

## Why a 5-day weekly template?

- It mirrors how most professionals already think about their week
- It's small enough to scan at a glance, big enough to capture a real schedule
- It pairs well with a daily template for high-stakes days
