## What's included

- **Daily** sheet — hour-by-hour single-day planner

- All in **one editable .xlsx file**

## How to use the template

1. **Download the .xlsx** and open it in your spreadsheet app of choice.
2. **Pick a tab** — daily, weekly, or monthly.
3. **Add fixed commitments** to the hourly rows.
4. **Color-code categories** using cell fill colors.
5. **Duplicate the file** each week so you always start fresh.

## Excel vs Google Sheets vs PDF

- **Excel** — best for offline work, formulas, and people already in Microsoft 365
- **Google Sheets** — best for collaboration and mobile-first editing
- **PDF** — best for printing and hand-writing
