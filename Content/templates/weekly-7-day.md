## Why plan all 7 days?

A 5-day workweek view hides what happens on weekends — when many of us actually do the rest, hobbies, errands, and family time that keep us functional. Blocking all 7 days helps you:

- **Protect rest** so weekends don't fill with chores
- **Balance work and life** across the whole week, not just Monday-Friday
- **See momentum** on hobbies, side projects, and personal goals
- **Plan recovery** around demanding workdays

## How to use this template

1. **Print one sheet per week.**
2. **Add fixed commitments** — work meetings, gym, family events, faith services.
3. **Block your most important work** during peak energy mornings.
4. **Schedule weekend priorities** — rest, hobbies, errands, family time.
5. **Review on Sunday** before printing next week.
