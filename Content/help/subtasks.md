Subtasks allow you to break down large time blocks into smaller, manageable steps. Whether you're planning a complex project or a simple morning routine, subtasks help you track progress and ensure every detail is accounted for.

## What Are Subtasks?

Subtasks are individual checklist items nested within a time block. They provide a clear roadmap of what needs to be done during that specific time slot. As you complete each step, you can check it off to visualize your progress.

## How to Add Subtasks

You can add subtasks to any time block from the **Time Block Editor**:

1. Tap on a time block in your timeline to open the editor.
2. Scroll down to the **Subtasks** section.
3. Tap **Add subtask**.
4. Type the title of your subtask.
5. Tap **return** on your keyboard to quickly add another subtask, or tap away to finish.

## Managing Your Subtasks

### Marking as Complete
To mark a subtask as complete, tap the circle next to its title. The subtask will be shown with a strikethrough and a green checkmark to indicate it's finished.

### Reordering Subtasks
If you need to change the order of your steps:
1. Press and hold the reorder icon (three lines) on the right side of a subtask.
2. Drag the subtask to its new position.
3. Release to save the new order.

### Deleting Subtasks
To remove a subtask:
1. Tap the **trash icon** (or the minus button) next to the subtask you want to remove.
2. The subtask will be immediately removed from the list.

## Tracking Progress on the Timeline

Align makes it easy to see how you're doing without opening the editor:

- **Progress Counter**: Your time blocks display a counter (e.g., "1/3") on the timeline, showing how many subtasks are completed out of the total.
- **Visual Cues**: When subtasks are present, a checklist icon appears on the time block to indicate there are smaller steps inside.

## Smart Completion Behavior

Align includes intelligent behavior to keep your schedule accurate:

**Automatic Parent Completion**: When you check off the final subtask in a list, Align automatically marks the entire parent time block as completed. This saves you from having to tap twice once your work is done.

## Subtasks and Recurring Events

Subtasks work seamlessly with recurring events:
- **Consistent Steps**: If you add subtasks to a recurring event series, those steps will appear in every future occurrence.
- **Per-Occurrence Customization**: You can add, edit, or check off subtasks for a specific day without affecting the rest of the series. This is perfect for daily routines where the core steps stay the same but small details might change.

## Common Questions

**Do subtasks have their own start and end times?**  
No, subtasks exist within the timeframe of their parent time block. They are meant to be steps performed during that scheduled period.

**Is there a limit to how many subtasks I can add?**  
There is no hard limit to the number of subtasks you can add, but we recommend keeping them concise for better readability on your timeline.

**Can I add subtasks to calendar events from other apps?**  
Yes! Align allows you to add subtasks to any item on your timeline, including events imported from Apple Calendar or Google Calendar.
