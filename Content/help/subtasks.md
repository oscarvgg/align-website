title: Subtasks
---

Subtasks let you track checklist-style steps inside a time block.

## What Subtasks Are

A subtask is a checklist item attached to a time block. Each subtask has a title and a completion state.

## Add Subtasks

1. Tap a time block to open the editor.
2. Scroll to the **Subtasks** section.
3. Tap **Add subtask**.
4. Enter a title.
5. Press **Return** to move to the next subtask.

## Edit and Complete Subtasks

### Mark a subtask as complete
Tap the circle next to the subtask.

### Remove a subtask
Tap the remove button next to the subtask.

### Reorder subtasks
If the editor shows drag handles, drag a subtask to reorder it.

## Progress on the Timeline

When a time block has subtasks:

- A checklist icon appears on the time block.
- A counter like `1/3` appears next to the icon (completed / total).

## Time Block Completion

When you save a time block with all subtasks completed, Align also marks the time block as completed.

## Recurring Time Blocks

If you add subtasks to a recurring time block, those subtasks are part of the series. Completion is tracked per occurrence.

## FAQs

### Do subtasks have their own times?
No. Subtasks are steps inside the parent time block.

### Is there a limit?
There is no hard limit, but shorter lists are easier to scan.
