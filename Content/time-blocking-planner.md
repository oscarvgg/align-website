# Time Blocking Planner

If you’ve tried to plan your day with a to‑do list and it still feels chaotic, a **time blocking planner** can help. Time blocking turns your priorities into a schedule by assigning real time to real work.

## What is a time blocking planner?

A time blocking planner is a planning system where you:
- decide what matters most,
- assign it a start and end time,
- and follow the plan (while staying flexible when life happens).

## Why Align works well as a time blocking planner

Align is designed around a visual timeline so you can see your full day at a glance:
- **Visual timeline** to plan your day in time blocks (not just lists).
- **Calendar sync** so your meetings and commitments are part of the same plan.
- **Recurring time blocks** to build routines without re-creating tasks.
- **Notifications** so you know when it’s time to start.
- **Overlap avoidance** to prevent double-booking your day.
- **Sync across devices** (iPhone, iPad, and Mac) with iCloud.

## How to start time blocking (quick steps)

1) Add your fixed commitments (meetings, appointments).
2) Block time for your most important tasks.
3) Add buffers for transitions and the unexpected.
4) Review once mid-day and adjust your blocks as needed.
