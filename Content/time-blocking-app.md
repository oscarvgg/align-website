## What is a time blocking app?

Time blocking is the method of planning your day by assigning a time window to each task. A time blocking app helps you:
- plan your day visually,
- adjust quickly when plans change,
- stay on track with reminders.

## Why Align is built for time blocking

Align gives you a clear timeline view of your day:
- **Visual timeline** that’s fast to scan.
- **Drag, resize, and reorganize** blocks when your day changes.
- **Calendar integration** so events and tasks live together.
- **Recurring blocks** for routines and habits.
- **Notifications** for smooth transitions.
- **Overlap prevention** so you don’t schedule two things at once.
