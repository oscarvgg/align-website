Why a monthly time blocking template?

A daily or weekly template helps you decide what to do today and this week. A monthly template zooms out:

  • See your month at a glance — busy weeks, recovery weeks, deadlines
  • Theme each week — deep work, admin catch-up, travel, launch prep
  • Track habits — mark exercise, learning, or writing days to spot streaks
  • Plan around big rocks — exams, launches, trips, family events

Use it alongside a daily or weekly template, not instead of one.

How to use this template

  1. Print one sheet per month.
  2. Mark milestones and deadlines first — launches, exams, trips.
  3. Theme each week with a single focus word.
  4. Block recurring habits across the grid.
  5. Review at month end before printing next month.
