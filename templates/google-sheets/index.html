How to use the template

  1. Click the link above — Google Sheets will prompt you to "Make a copy" into your Drive.
  2. Add fixed commitments to the hourly rows.
  3. Color-code by category (deep work, meetings, personal, rest).
  4. Duplicate the sheet each week so you always start from a fresh template.
  5. Share with your team if you plan collaboratively.

Google Sheets vs Excel vs PDF

  • Google Sheets — best for collaboration, mobile access, and auto-save
  • Excel — best for offline work and advanced formulas
  • PDF — best for printing and hand-writing
